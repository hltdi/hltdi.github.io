HLTDI

HLTDI is a research group for human language technology and the democratization of information. We work, more or less, on natural language processing tools for under-represented languages, focusing these days on machine translation. You can pronounce "HLTDI" like "HuLaTeDi".

What are you doing?

Oh, a few different things. On this page, we'll collect links to our different open source repositories.

  • L3, our machine translation system based on dependency grammars (code for morphological analyzers is also here)
  • Cross-lingual word sense disambiguation -- tools for lexical choice in hybrid machine translation systems
  • webapps for collecting corpora for under-resourced languages too
  • Other projects might collect on our github organization page...
  • ... and also on Michael Gasser's research software page.
